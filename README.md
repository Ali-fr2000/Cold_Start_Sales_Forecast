## Overview
This repository is developed as part of an MSc Thesis research initiative focusing on Supply Chain Analytics and Demand Forecasting. The pipelines are designed to solve the **"cold-start" forecasting problem** in retail and e-commerce. When new products are launched, they lack historical sales data, making traditional time-series forecasting ineffective.
